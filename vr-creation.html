How It Works

From idea
to heirloom.

We keep the process simple so you can focus on the vision— we handle the craft. Every order is a collaboration.

01

Share Your Vision

DM us on Instagram with your idea—a sketch, reference image, or even just a feeling. We'll ask the right questions.

02

Design Approval

We digitise your concept into a stitch-ready design. You review and approve before a single needle touches fabric.

03

Handcrafted Production

Our artisans bring the design to life stitch by stitch, using premium threads and materials chosen for lasting quality.

04

Delivered to You

Your finished piece is quality-checked, carefully packaged, and shipped—ready to be worn, gifted, or framed.
